Matrix is a lot of things, but a good Governor isn't any one of those things.

Throughout his term, Governor Matrix has repeatedly intervened in department affairs to make himself feel like an omnipotent God that can control everyone's employment at the click of a button. He has repeatedly made decisions the benefit himself or his friends, while hurting the people of Ridgeway that actually play the game and work hard for their positions. His administration is rewarded not by good work but by respect and loyalty to himself, not to the People of Ridgeway. Countless grievences have turned many members of the community against him, and this website aims at compiling all these incidents together.
